Privacy Policy App Name: Bird Bounce Developer: Hassan Tariq Contact Email: mhassan.tariq1998.dev@gmail.com Introduction Bird Bounce is a single-player mobile game built using Unity. The game is designed for users of all ages and does not require account creation, login, or any form of direct user identification. This privacy policy explains how information is handled when you use the app. Information Collection Bird Bounce does not collect personal information directly from users. However, the app uses third-party services that may automatically collect limited, non-personal data for analytics, crash reporting, and advertising purposes. Types of Data Collected The following data may be collected automatically by third-party services used in the app: Device information (such as device model and operating system version) App usage data (such as session duration and feature interactions) Crash logs and performance diagnostics Advertising data required to display non-personalized (contextual) ads Game progress data (such as coins, scores, or settings) is stored locally on the user’s device and is not linked to any personal identity. Purpose of Data Usage The collected data is used solely to: Improve app performance and stability Analyze gameplay behavior to enhance user experience Detect and fix crashes and technical issues Display non-personalized advertisements Advertising Bird Bounce displays non-personalized (contextual) advertisements. Non-personalized ads do not use advertising identifiers or user profiling and are shown based on factors such as app content, device type, or general location. Third-Party Services Bird Bounce uses the following third-party services: Google Firebase (analytics and crash reporting) Google AdMob (non-personalized advertising) These services may collect information in accordance with their own privacy policies. Data Sharing The developer does not sell, trade, or rent user data. Data is shared only with the third-party services listed above and only for functionality related to analytics, stability, and advertising. Children’s Privacy Bird Bounce is designed for a general audience, including children. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. All ads shown are non-personalized and compliant with child-directed content requirements. Data Security Reasonable technical measures are taken to protect the limited data processed by third-party services from unauthorized access or misuse. User Rights and Data Deletion Users may request information about or deletion of any collected data by contacting the developer at the email address provided above.
