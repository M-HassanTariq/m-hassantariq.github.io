Data Deletion Request

Bird Bounce is a single-player game and does not require user accounts. The game does not directly collect personal information.

What data may exist

Limited non-personal data may be collected automatically by third-party services used in the app, such as:

  • App usage analytics
  • Crash and performance diagnostics
  • Advertising data for non-personalized ads

How to request data deletion

If you would like to request deletion of data associated with your use of Bird Bounce, please contact the developer at:

Email: mhassan.tariq1998.dev@gmail.com

In your request, please include:

  • The name of the app (Bird Bounce)
  • Your device model
  • The approximate date you installed the app

Requests will be processed as soon as possible. Data handled by third-party services will be deleted according to their respective policies.
